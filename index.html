How to Fight Your Seasonal Allergies

Are you tired of allergies? Well these lists of helpful tips and tricks will inform you how to lessen your allergy triggers, even in the allergy season.

What to Avoid Eating
1.Refined Carbohydrates such as white bread and pastries
2.Fried Foods such as French Fries
3.Sugar Sweetened Beverages, soda and energy drinks
4.Red Meat, such as burgers and steak
5.Processed Meat, such as hot dogs and sausages
6.Fat Heavy spreads such as butter
While the following foods don’t directly exacerbate your allergies, they have been known to cause inflammation—one of the underlying causes of seasonal allergies
Reduce Exposure to Allergy Triggers
1. Stay indoors on dry, windy days.
2.Avoid lawn mowing, weed pulling and other gardening chores that stir up allergens.
3.Remove clothes you've worn outside and shower to rinse pollen from your skin and hair.
4.Don't hang laundry outside, pollen can stick to sheets and towels.
5.Wear a mask if you do outside chores
Avoiding these activities may help reduce your allergy attacks and improve your quality of life during allergy season and throughout the year.
